Our Services

Why you choose 4Z for Quality

Consulting

We create a quality culture throughout your organization, not merely building a quality management system, and we guide all our clients step by step, in changing their businesses from day-to-day success, into a continual and sustainable success.

Licensing Support

We assist established and new companies, pharmacies and traders during license issuance and renewal, in order to meet requirements. We help in defining policy, vision and mission statements, and in reshaping portfolios to attract customers.

ISO Qualification

We carefully observe your existing quality management system, and inspect it according to the international ISO Standards, to see if it is qualified for formal ISO certification, through a hassle-free process, for the lowest cost.

Strategic Planning

We help all our clients, whether companies or non-profits, in carrying out professional strategic planning, that is, designing and executing a clear roadmap, where goals are set, and all resources are provided, to reach these goals smoothly.

GAP Assessment

We offer this service to businesses, who are already certified, but would like to outsource internal audit, or have a QMS but not sure if it is good enough for certification, or those who need a full audit tool just to conduct self-assessment.

People Development

We take great care of people, the main core of any change and development. Therefore, we provide special services within our consultancy packages to train your workforce according to the latest knowledge base and quality standards.

Educational accreditation support

We are proud of our consultant’s competence for their long international academic experience, we professionally handle everything related to educational quality and accreditation in schools and universities according to international standards.

Biosafety Quality Consulting

Challenges posed globally by Corona pandemic increased the awareness and interest in biosafety. We have global and local experience in that field. Therefore, we provide consulting and training that guarantees efficient and cost-effective measures.

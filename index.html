OUR SERVICES

We proudly provide all the following consulting & training services for private businesses, public institutions, & non-profit organizations. In Bahrain, the GCC (Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, & UAE), & the Middle East (Egypt, Turkey, Iraq, & Libya).

Quality Consulting

we not only build a quality management system, rather we create a quality culture throughout the working place. We step by step guide our clients, in changing their goal from a day-to-day success, into a continual & sustainable success.

ISO Qualification

we carefully observe your existing quality management system. We inspect it according to the latest international ISO standards. To see if it is qualified for ISO certification. Through a hassle-free process, for the lowest cost.

Educational accreditation support

we are proud of our consultant’s competence & their long international academic experience. We professionally offer educational quality & accreditation consulting for schools & universities, based on relevant international standards.

Biosafety Quality Consulting

challenges posed by Covid-19 pandemic have increased the global awareness & interest in biosafety. We have proficient experts in this field. We provide consulting & training for the most efficient & cost-effective biosafety measures.

Human Resources Development

we take great care of people; the main core of any change & development. We provide special services within our consultancy packages. To train the workforce according to the latest knowledge & most updated quality standards.

Licensing Support

we assist new & established firms, pharmacies & traders during license issuance or renewal in meeting statutory requirements. We re-define your policy, vision & mission statements, & reshape portfolios to attract customers.

Strategic Planning

we support our clients in carrying out professional strategic planning. That is, designing & executing a clear roadmap. Where goals are set & all needed resources are provided. To reach these goals quickly & smoothly.

GAP Analysis

we offer this service to clients, who are certified, but would like to outsource internal audits, or those who have a QMS but not sure if it is good enough for certification, or those who need a full audit just to conduct a self-assessment.

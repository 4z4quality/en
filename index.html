Our SERVICES

We provide all the following consulting & training services for private businesses, public institutions, & non-profit organizations. In Bahrain, the GCC (Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, & UAE), & Middle East (Egypt, Turkey, Iraq, & Libya)

Quality Consulting

We create a quality culture throughout your organization, not merely building a quality management system. We guide our clients step by step, in changing businesses from day-to-day success, into a continual, sustainable success

Licensing Support

We assist established and new companies, pharmacies and traders in meeting requirements during license issuance and renewal. We re-define your policy, vision and mission statements, and reshape portfolios to attract customers.

ISO Qualification

We carefully observe your existing quality management system. We inspect it according to the international ISO Standards. To see if it is qualified for a formal certification. Through a hassle-free process, and for the lowest cost.

Strategic Planning

We support our clients, in carrying out professional strategic planning. That is, designing and executing a clear roadmap. Where goals are set, and all needed resources are provided. To reach these goals quickly and smoothly.

GAP Analysis

We offer this service to businesses, who are certified, but would like to outsource internal audit. Or have a QMS but not sure if it is good enough for certification. Or those who need a full audit tool just to conduct a self-assessment.

Human Resources Development

We take great care of people; the main core of any change and development. We provide special services within our consultancy packages. To train your workforce according to the latest knowledge and most updated quality standards.

Educational accreditation support

We are proud of our consultant’s competence and their long international academic experience. We professionally offer educational quality and accreditation consulting. For Schools and universities, based on international standards.

Biosafety Quality Consulting

Challenges posed by Covid-19 pandemic increased global awareness and interest in biosafety. We have international expertise in this field. We provide consulting and training for most efficient and cost-effective biosafety measures.
